## Protocol

- Data: `ml_model_data_with_predictions.parquet` (100,000 rows) → Step 10 preprocessing (80,924 rows) → SMOTE (83,380 rows; High: 16,676, Low: 16,676, Medium: 16,676, Very High: 16,676, Very Low: 16,676)
- Split: stratified 80/20, seed 42 (Step 08) — SMOTE is applied before the split, mirroring the notebook pipeline exactly
- Pipelines: **A** includes `current_energy_efficiency`; **B** excludes it and re-adds `energy_consumption_current`, `co2_per_m2`, `energy_per_m2` (Step 11)
- Class labels are the categorical codes: `0` = Very Low, `1` = Low, `2` = Medium, `3` = High, `4` = Very High
- XGBoost is fitted outside skore to preserve the notebook's early-stopping watchlist, so its report is test-only (no fit-time row)
- The DNN (Step 13) is excluded, matching the notebook's own Step 15 evaluation
- Metrics are skore's multiclass defaults (no manual `scoring` overrides)
